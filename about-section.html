About Us

The partnership between EssilorLuxottica, Ray-Ban's parent company, and Facebook to create the first generation of Ray-Ban Stories was publicly announced on September 20, 2020, by CEO Mark Zuckerberg during the seventh annual Facebook Connect conference.[7] During the keynote video, Zuckerberg described several new Facebook innovations, such as the Oculus Quest 2, a new augmented reality division called Project Aria, and the Ray-Ban Stories themselves

On September 9, 2021, Facebook launched Ray-Ban Stories, which were touted as the company's first product related to its plans for a metaverse

At Ray-Ban, we revolutionize the fusion of iconic style and cutting-edge technology with our Meta Smart Glasses. Since our inception, we have been synonymous with quality and fashion-forward eyewear. Our latest innovation, Meta Smart Glasses, pushes the boundaries of traditional eyewear by seamlessly integrating advanced tech features into timeless designs.

Our mission is to provide our customers with the best of both worlds: stylish eyewear that enhances their everyday lives. What Makes Ray-Ban Meta Smart Glasses Unique?

  1. Design Excellence: True to our heritage, the Meta Smart Glasses are meticulously crafted to ensure they remain a staple in contemporary fashion. They boast the same classic Ray-Ban silhouettes that have made our brand a global sensation.
  2. Smart Connectivity: With built-in Bluetooth 5.3 technology, our smart glasses ensure stable and low-latency audio transmission, making them perfect for listening to music, taking calls, and staying connected on the go.
  3. Enhanced Vision: Featuring high-definition lenses with anti-UV properties, our glasses protect your eyes from harmful rays while providing crystal-clear vision.
  4. Touch-Enabled Controls: Equipped with intuitive touch interface controls, the Meta Smart Glasses allow for effortless interaction, ensuring you can stay in control without missing a beat.
